--- layout: post title: Elegant Rest Services With Clojure date: 2014-03-18 10:14:17 UTC updated: 2014-03-18 10:14:17 UTC comments: false categories: clojure rest web services --- After spending some time in building Restful Web services with different frameworks and languages, i began to think that the Clojure solution is the most elegant and loosely coupled one. Instead of providing frameworks for handling everything, Clojure ecosystem has a bunch of libraries that you can put together, and along with the power of lisp, you can bend your rest services into DSL's for your application logic.

I will walk you through a couple of code snippets to show you how i implement rest services.

We will be using ring, compojure and http-kit to implement a clean, fast and elegant web service for building a simple pretty print service for edn files.
